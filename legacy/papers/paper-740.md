### Abstract

A new manifestation of technological revolution is rebuilding human civilization. At the dawn of upcoming era, large number of robust technologies like big data analytic, cloud computing, drones, AI-powered real time tools are conquering the global environment. Presently, artificial intelligence mechanisms are blended into our day-to-day life with an objective of enhancing human consciousness. Innovations are tailored in almost all the fields like education, health-care,political campaigns, cultural exchanges, socio-economic reformations, environmental safety etc.. In traditional frameworks, mathematical paradigms were constructed to come across scientific issues exclusively. Unfortunately, it failed on the part of social scientists to re- design global equations that could solve complex social glitches. The study carried out in this paper throws light on the social vectors that are highly influenced by the invasion of new frontiersof AI-enabled mechanisms. A Deep Transfer Learning model is employed to estimate the expected acceptance rate of AI technologies in the community. © 2023 IEEE.
