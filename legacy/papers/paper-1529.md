### Abstract

This paper proposes P-MADDPG, a trajectory prediction-based vehicular edge computing offloading algorithm that integrates trajectory prediction and multi-agent deep reinforcement learning. The trajectory prediction module uses a multilayer perceptron, self-attention, and a feedforward network to forecast vehicle positions and speeds, enhancing offloading accuracy. The reinforcement learning module optimizes power allocation, channel use, and computing resources through agent collaboration, guided by a reward function considering latency and power. Experiments show that P-MADDPG significantly reduces delay and improves resource utilization compared to traditional methods. © 2025 IEEE.
