### Abstract

In an islanded micro-grid, due to the power supply ability of distributed generation (DG) is limited, it can not always meet the power demand of all loads at the same time in a certain period of time. Therefore, the control of load becomes extremely important. In this paper, a coordinated control structure of microgrid based on multi-agent system is proposed, and the goal of distributed coordinated control of microgrid is given. Based on this, hierarchical management of load and coordinated control of energy in the microgrid with multiple DGs is proposed. When running in the island mode, the output power of the DGs is unable to meet the load demand, the strategy can realize hierarchical management of the load, and preferentially remove load with a lower priority to ensure the normal power supply of the sensitive load; At the same time, the output power of the DGs can be adjusted in real time according to the change of the load, so that the energy intelligent optimization distribution can be realized. Finally, the effectiveness of the proposed control strategy is verified by the microgrid model in the SIMULINK platform and the multi agent system simulation in the JADE platform. © 2018 IEEE.
