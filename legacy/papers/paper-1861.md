### Abstract

With the rise of intensive applications in the Internet of Vehicles (IoV), efficient task offloading is increasingly critical. However, mobility and heterogeneity of vehicles make optimization challenging. This study addresses task offloading in the IoV using edge computing with nearby vehicles. A multi-agent reinforcement learning strategy and vehicle pre-selection mechanism are proposed to optimize offloading, improving response time and energy efficiency. © 2025 IEEE.
