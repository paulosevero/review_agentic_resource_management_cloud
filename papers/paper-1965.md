### Abstract

Generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) is transforming agribusiness by enhancing research, optimizing production systems, and promoting sustainability. This chapter explores the applications of GenAI in agricultural research and development, including crop yield prediction, disease detection, breeding programs, and resource management. GenAI's integration with remote sensing, Internet of Things, and Edge Computing enables real-time data processing and decision-making, increasing efficiency and resilience in agricultural operations. Furthermore, its role in environmental monitoring, carbon footprint reduction, and supply chain optimization demonstrates its potential to address global challenges such as food security and climate change. Despite its promising applications, challenges related to model interpretability, data privacy, and infrastructure remain. The chapter concludes with future perspectives on GenAI's integration with advanced technologies, outlining its strategic role in the development of sustainable, intelligent, and inclusive agricultural systems. © 2026 Elsevier Inc.
