### Abstract

Deploying AI/ML models in enterprise-grade applications requires an inference infrastructure that is cost-effective, scalable, and responsive to variable workloads. This paper presents a modular and platform-agnostic inference architecture adopted at Salesforce to support generative AI use cases such as Agentforce and ApexGuru. The system integrates serverless execution, dynamic autoscaling, and MLOps pipelines to deliver consistent low-latency inference while optimizing GPU resource utilization. Case studies demonstrate performance gains exceeding 50% in tail latency and up to 4× throughput improvements compared to traditional static deployments. Designed with cloud neutrality and operational resilience in mind, this architecture enables organizations to deploy, scale, and govern large AI models efficiently in production. © 2025 IEEE.
