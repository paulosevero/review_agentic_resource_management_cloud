**Abstract**

> The problem of traffic congestion in urban mobility is still a great challenge that brings increased travel time, fuel consumption, and environmental pollution. Therefore, traditional systems of traffic management are server-centric using centralized cloud-based processing, leading to latency and precluding real-time decision-making. This paper presents a novel Edge AI-driven Smart Traffic Management System that predicts and optimizes traffic based on real-time deep learning. Taking advantage of the fact that there are already sensors and cameras everywhere, the system employs distributed edge computing, where squeezed into the edge of the traffic will be little AI-powered edge nodes processing real-time traffic data from cameras, sensors, and IoT devices, making ultra-fast congestion detection possible. The prediction of dynamic traffic flow is handled by a dynamic traffic flow prediction model based on the transformer deep learning model combined with the LSTM, and the signal control and rerouting strategies are optimized in real time with multi-agent deep reinforcement learning (MARL). In addition, an emergency response mechanism based on swarm intelligence guarantees some critical situations, e.g., accidents or VIP convoys are handled without a problem. The salient feature of blockchain-powered smart contracts is to enhance the security and transparency of the data sharing between edge nodes. Experimental results show that effectiveness in terms of traffic flow efficiency, congestion reduction, and computational latency can be much improved when using such systems as compared to traditional systems. It proposes a scalable and especially privacy-preserving and also energy-efficient solution for the next generation of smart city traffic management.
