### Abstract

This paper addresses the issue that excessive noise in rail transit affects passengers' comfort and audio-visual experience. Given the current situation where the active noise reduction function of headphones needs improvement, the innovation focuses on the two-way perception of headphones. That is, let the headphones respond to both environmental and content needs simultaneously, breaking through the single-point noise reduction thinking. It is envisioned that in the future, an intelligent audio-visual noise reduction system integrating big data, AI, and intelligent agent technology will be optimized to achieve specialized noise elimination for different film and television programs. This innovative concept is expected to partially solve the noise problem in rail transit, enhance users' riding experience, and bring about business model innovation and certain economic benefits. © 2025 IEEE.
