### Abstract

The meeting point of AI and Cloud Computing is changing the way we approach web development as we allow applications to scale and adapt to meet web's demand in ways we haven't been able to before. This article delves into how data-driven generative AI is revolutionizing the next-generation web development process in the US. Through integrating AI models like GPT, Codex and DALL•E with cloud-native platforms, including AWS, Azure and Google Cloud, developers are now seeing significant uplift in performance, efficiency and deployment agility. Empirical data collected from 150 tech companies operating in the USA show that with generative AI, deployment speeds surge 4x, developer productivity jumps 65% and content generation workflows run 3.5x faster. Additionally, a real-world impact is also demonstrated in the 80% cut in manual testing and 70% improved real-time user personalization accuracy that is realized in AI-enriched development environments. The work also identifies a reduction of 60% in infrastructure costs by BRAIN intelligent cloud orchestration, and 92% of surveyed organizations planning investment in AI-cloud strategies until 2026. By means of use case examples and performance test, we provide a full-fledged framework using generative AI and the cloud computing to develop intelligent, responsive, and scalable web applications that support emerging user requirements in the digital economy. © 2025 IEEE.
