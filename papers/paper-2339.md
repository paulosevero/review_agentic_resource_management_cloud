### Abstract

This paper presents a structured decoding approach to support reliable and efficient container orchestration using large language models (LLMs) in conjunction with the Model Context Protocol (MCP), a standard interface for LLMs to interact with Docker and Kubernetes. We address key challenges in using LLMs for container orchestration: high token overhead from outputs and the risk of generating invalid or unsafe commands. Empirical results demonstrate up to a 76.2% latency reduction.  © 2025 Copyright held by the owner/author(s).
