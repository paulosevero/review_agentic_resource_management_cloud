**Abstract**

> Artificial Intelligence (AI) workforce development is critical to national security and economic development. The rapid evolution of AI technologies has introduced curriculum innovation and resource challenges for high education institutions to offer AI education and scalable workforce training. One of such challenges is the demand of the affordable sharing of GPU resources for large language modeling and generative AI course delivery. We developed a web accessible Kubernetes based learning platform that can address GPU sharing challenges for AI education. This learning platform integrated various AI/ML frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Generative models to allow users to submit their AI tasks to access the GPU resources. Where Kubernetes schedules them as containerized jobs and channels the outcomes back through a messaging system. We have achieved the best performance, a GPU resource allocation algorithm considering load balance in our system design and implemented for efficient learning platform. These experimental studies were focused on determining how load balance could impact the task turnaround time and then evaluate the effectiveness of the GPU resource allocation algorithm to optimize the turnaround time for AI tasks.  © 2025 IEEE.
