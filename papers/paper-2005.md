### Abstract

This chapter explores the transformative impact of Generative AI across various industries, with a particular focus on how it revolutionizes key sectors such as healthcare, finance, cybersecurity, and IoT. Each section delves into specific applications, innovations, and challenges, demonstrating how Generative AI can automate processes, enhance systems, and lead to groundbreaking advancements in software engineering. Generative AI is transforming software engineering across multiple sectors, offering revolutionary innovations in automation, decision-making, and system optimization. This chapter explores the diverse applications of Generative AI in healthcare, finance, cybersecurity, and IoT. In healthcare, AI-driven diagnostics and personalized treatment plans enhance patient outcomes, while in finance, AI automates compliance and improves transaction security. In cybersecurity, Generative AI provides real-time threat detection and response mechanisms, and in IoT and edge computing, it enables smart systems to function with greater autonomy and efficiency. © 2025, IGI Global Scientific Publishing.
