### Abstract

Advanced Explorations in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and IoT examines how machine learning algorithms, computer vision techniques, and IoT infrastructures converge to create scalable, adaptive, and real-time intelligent systems. It balances rigorous theoretical foundations with system-level design, beginning with mathematical, probabilistic, and computational principles before moving into linear and nonlinear models, kernel methods, neural networks, deep learning architectures, and optimization strategies. The book highlights the integration of computer vision with IoT data pipelines, edge and cloud computing, wireless communication, and multi-agent systems, while also addressing advanced topics such as generative models, reinforcement learning, fuzzy intelligence, and explainable AI. Practical case studies in healthcare, smart environments, and autonomous systems illustrate real-world applications, making the text both a comprehensive theoretical resource and a guide to applied AI-driven IoT solutions.  © 2026, Bentham Books imprint.
