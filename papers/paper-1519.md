**Abstract**

> Cloud computing is one of the most promising technologies for online business services, scheduling real-time cloud jobs with excellent Quality of Service (QoS) remains a challenge with current methodologies. However, in reinforced rewards are typically unavailable until all jobs have been scheduled. In this paper, Multi-Component Attention Graph Convolutional Neural Network for QoS-Aware Cloud Job Scheduling and Resource Management Enhancing Efficiency and Performance in Cloud Computing (MCAGCN-QOS-CJS) is proposed. The cloud typically follows a long trajectory; to find trajectories with large rewards, resulting in concerns such as inefficient scheduling policies. Here, scheduling user requests improve job success rates and reduces response time. Initially, QoS based Job scheduling and resource management using Multi-Component Attention Graph Convolutional Neural Network (MCAGCN) to maximize Success Rates in the Cloud. However, MCAGCN does not include optimization strategies for determining optimal parameters for enhanced resource allocation in cloud computing. Hence Multi-Agent Cubature Kalman Optimizer (MACKO) is used to enhance weight parameters of MCAGCN. The performance measures including success rate, Average Response Time, Throughput, and Job Completion Time is considered. The MCAGCN-QOS-CJS method attains 23.10 %, 27.54 % and 25.72 %, higher Throughput, 21.48 %, 17.73 %, 25.32 % higher Utilization Rate and 20.98 %, 26.66 % and 16.32 % lower Average Response Time, 10.78 %, 20.47 %, and 12.86 % lower Job Completion Time are compared with existing techniques. © 2025
