### Abstract

Based on the analysis of the characteristics and requirements of the virtual workshop and the multi-agent system, this paper proposes the scheduling unit model of the multi-agent virtual workshop. According to this model, an improved genetic algorithm with simple modeling and fast computing speed is designed for scheduling, which can be used for scheduling problems of multiple projects in multiple virtual workshops with the same task type. © 2018 IEEE.
