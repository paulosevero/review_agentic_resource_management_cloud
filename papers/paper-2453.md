**Abstract**

> In the process of educational informatization, the demand for personalized learning continues to grow, and traditional standardized teaching content and fixed recommendation models can no longer meet learners' needs. This study proposes a personalized learning content generation and recommendation technical solution based on large language models. By deeply integrating large language models with knowledge graphs and combining an improved deep learning recommendation algorithm, it achieves intelligent learning content generation and precise recommendations. The research constructs a multi-level content generation framework, a personalized customization module, and a quality evaluation system. It also designs a user profile construction method based on multi-source data fusion and a deep interest network recommendation algorithm, while offering a solution to the cold start problem. Experimental results show that this technical solution has significant effectiveness in improving learning outcomes and optimizing user experience, providing a feasible technical path for advancing personalized learning development in the education field.  © 2025 IEEE.
