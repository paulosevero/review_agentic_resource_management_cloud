### Abstract

With more and more Digital Twins(DT) technology applied to smart cities, industrial internet of things and metaverse, the large number of real-time sensor data and the large number of multimodal holographical interaction bring new issues for dynamic resource scheduling of computing resources This paper puts forward a Holographic Resource Perception and Intelligent Agent Computing Power Dynamic Scheduling Model for Digital Twins: The model fuses multiple heterogeneous sources of sensing data with holoscopic perception techniques to produce an accurate representation of the environment, objects and interactions present in relation to a user A multi-agent collaborative decision making approach integrated with DRL strategies is used by the proposed model for dynamic optimization of computing resources allocation and migration among the layers at Edge-Cloud-End According to the experimental results, compared with traditional scheduling algorithms, the proposed algorithm achieves much more resource utilization, shorter task latency, and more robust system. Providing scalable intelligent computing power management for immersive DT application. © 2025 IEEE.
