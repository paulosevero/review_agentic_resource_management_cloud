### Abstract

With almost half of the returned goods finally destined for landfills, the global returns management crisis poses an 816 billion annual challenge with significant environmental and financial inefficiencies. This work presents a new circular supply chain architecture integrating edge computing, artificial intelligence, blockchain technology, and green analytics to revolutionize returns processing. Our proposed system comprises edge scanners for real-time condition assessment at drop-off points, artificial intelligence agents for intelligent routing decisions among restocking, recycling, and refurbishing paths, and blockchain technology for thorough environmental impact tracking. We show by empirical analysis and simulation modeling that this combined approach can recover 75% of the return value and cut Waste by 60%. By using IoT sensor networks, machine learning algorithms, distributed ledger technology, and sustainability measurements, this approach creates a complete solution for a circular economy. Compared to conventional linear returns management strategies, our results show notable increases in processing efficiency, environmental sustainability, and economic recovery. This study adds to the increasing corpus of knowledge on sustainable supply chain management. It offers a valuable structure for companies applying circular economy ideas in their return systems.  © 2025 IEEE.
