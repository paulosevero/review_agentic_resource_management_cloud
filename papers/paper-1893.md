### Abstract

Large language models (LLMs) like Chat-GPT are driving rapid growth in AI infrastructure, with computing demands scaling from hundreds of thousands to millions of GPUs. We explored the architecture and field trials of OCS-based DCN networks, as well as WDM-enabled multi-datacenter distributed training. Key technologies including OCS, CPO, LPO, optoelectronic devices, and ROADM networks were also discussed in the context of DCN and DCI requirements. © 2025 SPIE. All rights reserved.
