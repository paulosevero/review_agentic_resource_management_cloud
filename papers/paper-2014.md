**Abstract**

> Chapter 6 introduces the MAGIC-B© framework, which integrates six 4IR technologies—machine learning, artificial intelligence, generative AI, IoT, cloud computing, and blockchain—with the five phases of design thinking. Each phase is mapped to specific technologies that enhance insight, experimentation, and scaling. Case studies illustrate how an agricultural firm used IoT to gather empathic field data, a health company used generative AI during ideation, and a logistics firm used blockchain and machine learning for prototype testing and auditability. The model emphasizes modular, ethical deployment. Teams are encouraged to treat MAGIC-B© as a toolkit, selecting enablers based on user needs. The framework supports fast learning, co-creation, and scale. This chapter builds the bridge between human-centered innovation and tech-enabled execution. © 2026 Vijay Pandiarajan.
