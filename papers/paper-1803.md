**Abstract**

> To address the challenge of optimizing resource allocation in integrated sensing, communication, and computing (ISCC) networks under stochastic and diverse multi-target sensing demands. We integrate mobile edge computing (MEC) with integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) to realize ISCC, fully accounting for the resource competition between communication and sensing, and ensuring system stability. Furthermore, considering that the interference characteristics in sensing and communication are closely related to users spatial locations, we design a Lyapunov-guided multi-agent deep reinforcement learning algorithm with graph attention networks (LG-MADRL) to effectively capture spatial features and jointly optimize the allocation of sensing, communication, and computing resources in each time slot. Simulation results show that the proposed LG-MADRL method significantly enhances system utility and energy efficiency while ensuring task queue stability.  © 2025 IEEE.
