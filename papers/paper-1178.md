### Abstract

This study investigates the transformative potential of reinforcement learning algorithms in the context of dynamic decision-making for modern businesses. Deep Q-Networks (DQN), Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), Deep Deterministic Policy Gradients (DDPG), and Multi-Agent Deep Deterministic Policy Gradients (MADDPG) are the algorithms under investigation. The primary objective is to improve strategic decision-making procedures by using the power of very sophisticated algorithms housed inside a framework that is hosted on the cloud. The major goal is to assess and compare the performance of these algorithms in addressing the problems given by dynamic and complicated decision-making settings. This will be accomplished by analyzing and comparing the performance of these algorithms. The innovation is in the combination of cutting-edge reinforcement learning methods with the infrastructure of cloud computing, which enables scalability and agility to meet the changing demands of contemporary businesses. The research presents evidence that indicates how certain algorithms might improve decision-making processes, hence increasing agility and resilience in the face of uncertainty. © 2024 IEEE.
