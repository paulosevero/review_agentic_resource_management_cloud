### Abstract

The Multiple Intelligent Agent-assisted Coordination strategy in Cloud environment focus on devising and making of multiple intelligent agents to encourage the detection of appropriate cloud service, enabling smooth agreement between seller and provider, as well as Categorization of cloud services. This research work is most excellent initiation for effective Cloud resource management. The contribution of research work includes developing an agent-assisted cloud service searching tool for extracting suitable cloud services from large collection of cloud providers list. Cloud services agreement method facilitates the agreement process among shopper agent & negotiator agent as well as negotiator agent, & seller agent. In cloud service Categorization, the cloud service are classified as SaaS, IaaS, PaaS and so on for vigorously selects the Cloud services from different cloud providers. The proposed work of this research work is to compose variety of appropriate cloud services and provide unified virtualized service to cloud customers. © 2017 IEEE.
