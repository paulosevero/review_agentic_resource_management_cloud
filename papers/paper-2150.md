### Abstract

In this chapter, we delve into the analysis of renal CT images and how advanced technologies like fog computing (FC), Internet of Things (IoT), and hyperparameter optimization (HPO) can enhance machine learning (ML) models in smart healthcare systems. By incorporating FC and IoT, we can overcome challenges faced by traditional healthcare ML approaches, such as data processing, latency, and privacy concerns. We emphasize the importance of hyperparameter tuning in optimizing model performance, as evidenced by the segmentation results. Furthermore, we explore how IoT devices and FC architecture enable real-time data processing. HPO techniques are specially designed to improve segmentation accuracy. This covers practical challenges in creating and implementing ML models for analyzing kidney CT images, including preprocessing steps and model selection. The results demonstrate the effectiveness of our approach in real healthcare settings and suggest potential enhancements in patient outcomes and clinical decision-making. The significant impact of FC and IoT on healthcare delivery is emphasized in the concluding section, which also outlines future research directions and emerging trends in healthcare AI. © 2026 Elsevier Inc.
