### Abstract

We propose a connectivity provisioning scheme based on AI traffic pattern for cross-datacenter LLM training in optical transport networks (OTN). Simulations show that this method saves up to 40% of the bandwidth on the premise of guaranteeing the service quality.  ©2024 The Author(s)
