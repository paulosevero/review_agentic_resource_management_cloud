### Abstract

The development of new technologies and recent paradigms should be explored to support interoperability in the medium and long term. This paper examines the integration of micro-services and multi-agent systems (MAS), implementing a new paradigm called Multi-Agent Microservices (MAMS) for the dynamic composition of Semantic Web services. The objective is to improve certain agility in the development of the system and to facilitate the deployment of Multi-Agent Systems (MAS) within microservices. This approach uses a "dynamic"composition and must consider the optimization of the QoS (Quality of Service), the satisfaction of the user's global constraints, and the preservation of the semantic coherence of the data flow exchanged between the different composite services. To validate the proposed approach, we give an example of applied to tourism.  © 2020 ACM.
