### Abstract

The field of Edge Computing (EC) has emerged as a significant advancement that allows Internet of Things (IoT) devices to efficiently manage complex tasks while prolonging their operational lifespan. The focus of this study is on the utilization of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) to augment edge computing systems with the goal of minimizing energy usage during computational tasks. We propose a novel approach that employs multi-agent reinforcement learning to facilitate task offloading in a UAV-supported edge computing setup. This method swiftly devises offloading strategies without the protracted computations typically associated with conventional optimization algorithms. Through experimental trials, we have found that our proposed solution not only reduces the energy required for computing tasks but also enhances the overall efficiency of task processing and demonstrates robust performance stability. © 2024 IEEE.
