### Abstract

In this poster, the energy and carbon management problem in Data Center Microgrid (DCMG) is modeled as a Decentralized Partially Observable Markov Decision Process, and the Multi-Agent Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (MADDPG) algorithm is adopted to learn the optimal operating policy. In addition, the system uses blockchain and smart contract to ensure data security and auditability in the power trading process. Finally, simulation results show that the proposed system significantly reduces the overall cost of the DCMGs compared with traditional systems.  © 2024 IEEE.
