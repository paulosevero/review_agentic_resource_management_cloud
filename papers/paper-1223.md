### Abstract

This paper presents an attention mechanism-empowered multi-agent deep deterministic policy gradient (MADDPG) algorithm for distributed resource allocation in cell-free mobile edge computing (MEC) system. The proposed algorithm leverages the attention mechanism to adaptively weigh the contributions of other agents, outperforming the performance of conventional MADDPG algorithm. © 2024 IEEE.
