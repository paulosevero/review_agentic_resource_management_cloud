### Abstract

In this paper, a model predictive control (MPC)strategy is investigated for a cloud-integrated networked multiagent system (NMAS)under bandwidth allocation. The cloud-integrated NMAS is established in cloud computing and network environments. A Stackelberg game is exploited to describe the bandwidth allocation on the cloud-integrated NMAS. With effects on the bandwidth allocation, time delays are compensated by the MPC strategy which is based on a multirate method. Equilibrium solutions and stability conditions are given for the Stackelberg game and the cloud-integrated NMAS, respectively. Effectiveness of the proposed strategy is demonstrated by simulation results. © 2019
