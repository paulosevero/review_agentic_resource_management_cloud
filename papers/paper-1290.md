**Abstract**

> With the rise of edge computing, deploying computing resources to edge devices close to data sources has become a paradigm for providing low latency services. This study focuses on the micro-service chain placement problem, the dynamic topology of micro-service chains, the heterogeneity of edge servers, and the diverse resource requirements of micro-services make finding the optimal placement strategy a challenging task in a dynamic edge environment. By employing the Multi-Agent Proximal Policy Optimization (MA-PPO) algorithm, we model the micro-service chain placement process as a Markov decision process and utilize deep reinforcement learning to explore the optimal strategy. This study validates the proposed MA-PPO based micro-service chain placement method through extensive simulation experiments. © 2024 ACM.
